About Warner Bros Entertainment

Warner Bros., established in 1923, stands as an enduring pillar of the entertainment industry, renowned for its unparalleled contributions across film, television, and interactive media.


Boasting a legacy that spans nearly a century, Warner Bros. has continually redefined storytelling, innovation, and cultural impact.


From timeless cinematic masterpieces like "Casablanca" and "The Wizard of Oz" to contemporary juggernaut franchises such as "Harry Potter" and the iconic heroes of DC Comics, the studio's diverse portfolio resonates with audiences of all ages and backgrounds.


Beyond its cinematic triumphs, Warner Bros. has made indelible marks in television, bringing to life beloved series like "Friends," "The Big Bang Theory," and "Game of Thrones."Furthermore, its interactive division consistently delivers immersive gaming experiences that push the boundaries of creativity and technology.


As a global leader, Warner Bros. remains committed to fostering creativity, diversity, and innovation, ensuring its continued influence and relevance in shaping the future landscape of entertainment worldwide.
