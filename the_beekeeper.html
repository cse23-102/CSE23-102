The Beekeeper

The Beekeeper is a 2024 American action thriller film about Adam Clay (Jason Statham), a beekeeper who seeks revenge against those who scammed and killed his landlady, Eloise Parker (Phylicia Rashad). Clay is a former member of a classified program called Beekeepers who has never been fingerprinted and operates outside of the government. When Clay learns that Eloise has been targeted by scammers and robbed of her life savings, he sets out to find and burn down the call center responsible.
